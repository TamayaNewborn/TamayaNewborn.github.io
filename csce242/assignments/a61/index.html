RESEARCH

The Carnegie Foundation has designated the University of South Carolina as an institution of highest research activity, a label it bestows on doctoral degree granting institutions with large-scale research enterprises. Join the Office of the Vice President for Research in our search for practical solutions to create healthier, better educated communities.

___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Research Focus Areas >>

Faculty members from a diverse range of disciplines participate in research to benefit the people of South Carolina and our nation as well as tackle global challenges such as health care, the environment, alternative energy sources and the creation of advanced materials.

>

Faculty Expertise >>

Our faculty garnered more than $242 million in sponsored awards in 2015. We are the only institution in the state designated as having “very high research activity” by the Carnegie Foundation.

Business Partnership Opportunites >>

The Office of Economic Engagement connects businesses, industry and entrepreneurs to the many resources of the University of South Carolina to create jobs, accelerate innovation and drive economic prosperity in the state, region and around the world.

Faculties and Resourses >>

Our continuing investment in laboratories, equipment and facilities provides researchers access to resources across campus, from Longstreet Theatre to the Mass Spectrometry Lab. We provide all our researchers with support for every step of their work, from writing grant proposals to accounting, mentoring and administrative help.
